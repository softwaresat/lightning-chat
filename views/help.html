Help!

This guide has various answers to some frequent questions. To ask questions to staff and the development team, go to our discord server!

How to Change your Password

To change your password, go to Account in the dropdown on the top right corner of your screen and type your password in, you can then press save to save your changes!

How to Change your Profile Picture

To change your profile picture, go to Account in the dropdown on the top right corner of your screen, then press choose file and select the image. Finally you can press submit to save the profile picture! Note: The image must be in the .jpg format!

Texting Commands

Lightning Chat uses markdown formatting for all messages, so markdown in your message can change the appearance. Here are some of the most useful commands for you to know:
Bold - > "**text**" Ex. **This is bold**
Italicize - > "*text*" Ex. *This is italicized*
Big Text - > "# text" Ex. # This text is big, you know what, now that I'm thinking about it, this may be a bit too big
Medium Text - > "### text" Ex. ### This is medium text, you know, pretty average sizing
Small Text - > "###### text" Ex. ###### This is really small text, not sure if you can read it, but its totally fine!
Hidden Link - > "[Text](URL)" Ex. [Lightning Chat](https://www.lightningchat.live)
Code - > "``Text``" Ex. ``Cool, I can code now!``

How to Create a Chatroom

To create a chatroom, first press new chatroom in the navigation bar at the top. Then type the Name of the room, select if its a private room, needs a cursing filter, and if messages are deleted every 30 minutes.

How to View Other Chatrooms

To view other chatrooms, press chatrooms in the navigation bar at the top. Then you can press join to join the chatroom.

How to Enable Notifications

To enable notifications, first check to see if there's a button in the navigation bar above. If not, do ctrl+shift+r or cmd+shift+r, depend on what OS you are on, and then find the button at the top.

How to Download the Lightning Chat App

To download the app on iOS or iPadOS, go to lightningchat.live on Safari, then press the share button, scroll down and press Add to Home Screen.
To Download the app on Android, Windows, ChromeOS, or MacOS open a browser and go to lightningchat.live, then press the download button on the URL bar.
